Hey there! I'm Samuel Afon.

I was born in Nigeria on December 4, 2003, and moved to the U.S. in 2018. My creative journey kicked off on January 1, 2023, when I bought my first camera—and I haven't put it down since.

Currently, I'm a student at the University of North Texas (UNT) in Denton, where I'm studying Information Technology with a focus on web development. This site is a space where I blend my two passions—photography and web design.

When I'm not busy with school, you'll catch me behind the lens, shooting everything from sleek cars to stunning real estate, captivating portraits, and more.

Feel free to browse around and check out my work. If you like what you see, let's connect!
